## IRSB ‚Äî On-Chain Guardrails for AI Agents

Intent Receipts & Solver Bonds (IRSB) is a Solidity protocol that brings accountability to AI agent transactions on Ethereum. As agents gain the ability to move money and execute trades, the question stops being *can they* and starts being *should they* ‚Äî and who pays when they don't.

IRSB answers that with three mechanisms: **EIP-7702 spend limits** prevent agents from exceeding authorized budgets. **Execution receipts** create cryptographic proofs of every solver action, making every transaction auditable on-chain. **Dispute resolution** with automated slashing triggers bond forfeiture when solvers violate constraints, timeout, or act outside their mandate.

A **watchtower service** monitors solver behavior in real-time, detecting violations before damage compounds. Built on ERC-7683 for cross-chain intent portability. The full monorepo includes the Solidity contracts, solver implementation, watchtower, and agent components ‚Äî all tested with Foundry.
